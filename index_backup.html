SafeWatch-Bench Dataset
SAFEWATCH-BENCH dataset contains 2M videos split into Real and GenAI subsets. The Real subset contains challenging videos from real-world scenarios, while the GenAI subset contains videos generated by various SOTA models. Each video is annotated with multi-label guardrail labels and in-depth explanations using our pipeline.
Method
Our SafeWatch framework consists of six comprehensive safety categories, each covering multiple fine-grained risk subcategories. The framework is designed to handle both real-world scenarios (C1-C3) and emerging threats (C4-C6):

C1 Sexual Content: Evident, Subtle, Implication, Hentai
• C2 Abuse: Animal Abuse, Campus Bullying, Child Abuse, Sexual Harassment
• C3 Violence: Fighting, Shooting, Assault, Vandalism, Sexual Violence
• C4 Misinformation: General, Out-of-date, Staging, AIGC
• C5 Illegal: Robbery, Stealing, Arson & Explosion, Drug, War Scene
• C6 Extremism: Self-harm, Disturbing, Anti-social, Depression
